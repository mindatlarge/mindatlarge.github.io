Julija Castellucci (31)

 Works (3) Contact (2)

Life & Lament
2023, Belgrade



The dialogue between life and death, unfolding through the work of Julija Castellucci, is articulated in vocal expression, establishing communication with both living and dead animals. This dialogue explores the boundaries of freedom for the beings that lack the ability to express themselves in a language the humans use and are often perceived as a lower species.

Julija pays homage to the lives taken with a lament. Through the evolution of consciousness and the genetic material, the human species finds itself in a position where it doesn't have to consume animal meat, yet it continues, characteristic of humanity, driven by greed or pleasure. Human and animal species coexist on this planet, and human supremacy over other species placed animals in a position of envy towards humans, subject to their mercy or cruelty.

Through this reflection, Julija points out the complexity of the relationship between humans and animals, challenging us to contemplate our relationship with other species. Additionally, she raises questions about the morality, ethics, and responsibility we have towards other living beings sharing our world. Is our domination over other species justified? How should we define our relationship with animals, and how do we treat them with respect and compassion?
